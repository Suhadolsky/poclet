In 2026, the gap between a "PC handheld" and a "console handheld" has never been smaller. The Switch 2 now has the power to run modern AAA games, and the Steam Deck now has a "console-like" OS that doesn't require a keyboard and mouse. 

So, if you can only have one, do you choose the open freedom of a PC or the curated magic of Nintendo?
