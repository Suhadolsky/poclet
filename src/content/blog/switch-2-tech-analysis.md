We've spent two weeks with the **Nintendo Switch 2**, and one thing is clear: Nvidia has pulled off a miracle.

The custom **Tegra T239** chip (Ampere architecture) isn't just a mobile processor. It's a tiny RTX 3050 in your backpack.

## The Secret Weapon: DLSS 3.5 ðŸ§ 

This is where the magic happens.
In our tests with *Cyberpunk 2077: Switch Edition*, the console renders the game internally at **720p**.
But thanks to **DLSS (Deep Learning Super Sampling)**, it outputs a clean, reconstructed **1440p** image to your TV.

> **Digital Analysis:** The image quality looks *better* than the Xbox Series S in some scenes because DLSS reconstruction is superior to standard FSR upscaling used by AMD consoles.
