### 1. Joy-Con Wobble (Magnetic)
*   **Issue:** The new magnetic attachment feels loose on some units.
*   **Fix:** Nintendo is offering free replacement rails. Temporary fix: Apply a small strip of electrical tape to the contact point for a tighter fit.

### 2. Docking Station Scratches
*   **Issue:** The new metal dock can scratch the screen bezel.
*   **Fix:** Buy a screen protector immediately. Or apply felt tape inside the dock rails.

### 3. Wi-Fi 6E Dropouts
*   **Issue:** Constant disconnects on 6GHz networks.
*   **Fix:** Firmware update 2.1.0 fixed this. Update your console!
