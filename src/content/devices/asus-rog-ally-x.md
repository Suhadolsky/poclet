### 1. SD Card Reader Fried?
*   **Status:** The *original* Ally fried SD cards due to heat.
*   **Ally X Fix:** ASUS moved the SD slot away from the vent. It is **SAFE** on the Ally X.

### 2. Sticks Feel Loose
*   **Fix:** The Ally X uses new stick modules (tighter than original). If they feel loose, you can tighten the tension springs inside (requires opening) or increase deadzone in Armoury Crate.

### 3. Windows Updates Broke Something
*   **Fix:** Always update **MyASUS** and **Armoury Crate** first. Windows Update sometimes installs generic drivers that break special keys.
