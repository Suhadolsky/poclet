Not everyone needs a "pocket PC" with screaming fans and a two-hour battery life. If you have a high-speed internet connection and a Game Pass or GeForce Now subscription, a **Cloud Handheld** is the smart choice. 

In 2026, the two main competitors are the **Logitech G Cloud 2** and the **Razer Edge (2026 Edition)**. Here is the breakdown.
